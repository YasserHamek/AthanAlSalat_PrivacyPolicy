أذان الصلاة: Azan al-Salat - Politica da protecziun da datas

Ultima actualisaziun: Avrigl 2026

Resumà: أذان الصلاة: Azan al respects your privacy. We do not collect account-level personal data such as names, email addresses, or advertising identifiers. Some location-based features may send your GPS coordinates to third-party services to calculate prayer times or resolve city names.

Infurmaziuns che nus na recullein betg

أذان الصلاة: Azan al-Salat does not collect account-level personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Nums, adresas dad e-mail u infurmaziuns da contact
  • Identificaturs da l'apparat per il retracziament
  • Analitika d'utilisaziun u datas da cumpurtament
  • Naginas datas per fines da publicitad

Memorisaziun da datas locala

أذان الصلاة: Azan al-Salat stores the following data locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. These settings are saved on-device, although location-based features may also send your GPS coordinates to third-party services when needed:

  • Posiziun: Coordinatas GPS e num da la citad, memorisadas per il chaltgul e la visualisaziun da las uras da prieghiera
  • Parameters da prieghiera: Metoda da chaltgul, sons da l'athan, preferenzas per notificaziuns ed iqama
  • Cache da prieghiera: Cache offline da 30 dis per las uras da prieghiera
  • Quran: Preferenzas da translaziun, translaziuns en cache, segnapaginas e dimensiun dal font
  • Parameters da l'app: Lingua, moda notturna e status d'onboarding
  • Status d'acquists: Registraziun d'acquists entaifer l'app

Dumondas da rait

The following is the complete list of network requests the app may make. No direct identifiers such as your name, email address, or device ID are attached to these requests:

  • Aladhan API (aladhan.com) — Used as a fallback only if offline prayer calculation fails. Sends your GPS coordinates and calculation method to calculate prayer times.
  • AlQuran Cloud API (alquran.cloud) — Chatta translaziuns dal Quran vid l'emprim utilisader. Trametta mo il numer dal chapitel e il code da translaziun.
  • Photon / Nominatim / OpenStreetMap — Utilisà ina giada per convertir las vossas coordinatas GPS en in num da citad per la visualisaziun. Nagin identificatur persistent vegn tramess.
  • Controlla DNS (dns.google) — Ina consultaziun DNS standard utilisada en il fund per controllar la disponibilitad d'internet avant d'actualisar il cache da prieghiera. Naginas datas da l'applicaziun vegnan tramessas.

Acquists entaifer l'app

أذان الصلاة: Azan al-Salat offra acquists opziunals entaifer l'app, tractads cumpletamain tras il Google Play Store cun Google Play Billing. Las registraziuns d'acquists èn administradas da Google e memorisadas localmaing sin voss apparat.

Servetschs da terzas persunas

أذان الصلاة: Azan al-Salat n'integrescha nagins servetschs da terzas persunas per analitika, publicitad u rapports da craschs. I na exista nagin SDK d'analitica ni da publicitad. Il sulet servetsch extern utilisà per acquists è Google Play Billing.

Privatsfera dals uffants

أذان الصلاة: Azan al does not knowingly collect account-level personal information from children or adults.

Stizzament da datas

Perquai che tuttas datas èn memorisadas localmaing sin voss apparat:

  • La desinstalaziun da l'app stizza tuttas las datas memorisadas localmaing
  • Vus pudais resetter la posiziun e las preferenzas a partir da l'écran da parameters da l'app
