Inqubomgomo Yobumfihlo ye-أذان الصلاة: Azan al-Salat

Ibuyekeziwe okugcina: Ephreli 2026

Isifinyezo: أذان الصلاة: Azan al iyabuhlonipha ubumfihlo bakho. Asiqoqi idatha yomuntu siqu yeleveli ye-akhawunti njengamagama, amakheli e-imeyili, noma izihlonzi zokukhangisa. Ezinye izici ezisekelwe endaweni zingathumela izixhumanisi zakho ze-GPS ezinsizakalweni zezinkampani zangaphandle ukubala izikhathi zokuthandaza noma ukuxazulula amagama amadolobha.

Ulwazi Esingaziqoqi

أذان الصلاة: Azan al ayiqoqi imininingwane yomuntu siqu yeleveli ye-akhawunti. Ngokukhethekile, asiqoqi:

  • Amagama, izikheli ze-imeyili, noma ulwazi loxhumano
  • Izinombolo zokubona idivayisi ngezinjongo zokulandelela
  • Izitatistiki zokusetshenziswa noma idatha yezenzo
  • Noma yimuphi idatha ngezinjongo zokukhangisa

Ukugcinwa Kwedatha Endaweni

أذان الصلاة: Azan al igcina idatha elandelayo endaweni kudivayisi yakho isebenzisa i-Android SharedPreferences. Lezi zilungiselelo zilondolozwa kudivayisi, nakuba izici ezisuselwe endaweni zingase futhi zithumele izixhumanisi zakho ze-GPS kumasevisi ezinkampani zangaphandle uma kudingeka:

  • Indawo: Izintambo ze-GPS negama lomuzi, ezibekelelwe ukuze kubaliwe futhi kuboniswe izikhathi zomkhuleko
  • Izilungiselelo Zomkhuleko: Indlela yokubala, izwi le-adhaan, izaziso nezinto ezifunekayo ze-iqama
  • Isitoreji Somkhuleko: Isitoreji sezikhathi zomkhuleko esingaxhunyiwe ku-inthanethi sezinsuku ezingama-30
  • IQur'an: Izilungiselelo zokuhumusha, ukuhumusha okubekelelwe, izimpawu zezincwadi, nobukhulu bophawu
  • Izilungiselelo Zohlelo: Ulimi, imodi emnyama, nesimo sokwethulwa
  • Isimo Sokuthenga: Irekhodi lezokuthenga ngaphakathi kohlelo

Izicelo Zenethiwekhi

Okulandelayo uhlu oluphelele lwezicelo zenethiwekhi uhlelo lokusebenza olungazenza. Azikho izihlonzi eziqondile ezifana negama lakho, ikheli le-imeyili, noma i-ID yedivayisi enamathiselwe kulezi zicelo:

  • Aladhan API (aladhan.com) — Isetshenziswa njengempendulo kuphela uma ukubala komkhuleko ongaxhunyiwe ku-inthanethi kwehluleka. Ithumela izixhumanisi zakho ze-GPS nendlela yokubala ukubala izikhathi zomkhuleko.
  • AlQuran Cloud API (alquran.cloud) — Ilanda ukuhumusha kweQur'an ngokusetshenziswa kokuqala. Ithumela inombolo yesahluko negambiso lokuhumusha kuphela.
  • Photon / Nominatim / OpenStreetMap — Isetshenziswa kanye ukuguqula izintambo ze-GPS zakho zibe igama lomuzi ukuze kuboniswe. Akukho sihlonzi esiqhubekayo esithunyiwe.
  • Ukuhlola i-DNS (dns.google) — Ukubuza kwe-DNS okujwayelekile okusetshenziswa ngemuva kwezindlela ukuhlola ukutholakala kwe-inthanethi ngaphambi kokuvuselela isitoreji somkhuleko. Akukho idatha yohlelo ethunyiwe.

Ukuthenga Ngaphakathi Kohlelo

I-أذان الصلاة: Azan al-Salat inikeza ukuthenga okungabandlululwanga ngaphakathi kohlelo okuqhutshelwa ngokuphelele nge-Google Play Store usebenzisa i-Google Play Billing. Amarekhodi okuthenga aphelezelelwa ngu-Google futhi agcinwa endaweni kudivayisi yakho.

Izinsiza Zesithathu

I-أذان الصلاة: Azan al-Salat ayihlanganyeli noma yimuphi izitatistiki, ukukhangisa, noma izinsiza zokubika ukuwa kwesithathu. Ayikho i-SDK yokuhlaziya noma yokukhangisa. Insiza eyodwa yangaphandle esetshenziswa ukuthenga yi-Google Play Billing.

Ubumfihlo Babantwana

أذان الصلاة: Azan al ayiqoqi ngamabomu ulwazi lomuntu siqu lweleveli ye-akhawunti kusuka ezinganeni noma kubantu abadala.

Ukususwa Kwedatha

Njengoba yonke idatha igcinwa endaweni kudivayisi yakho:

  • Ukukhipha uhlelo kususa yonke idatha egcinwa endaweni
  • Ungasetha kabusha indawo nezilungiselelo kusuka esikrini sezilungiselelo sohlelo

Izinguquko Kule Nqubomgomo

Singabuyekeza le nqubomgomo yobumfihlo ngasikhathi. Noma yiziphi izinguquko zizobekwa kuleli khasi nge-akhawunti yosuku olubuyekeziwe.
