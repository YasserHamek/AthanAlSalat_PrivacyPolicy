أذان الصلاة: Athan al Salat Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Summary: This privacy policy applies to the Samsung Galaxy Store version of أذان الصلاة: Athan al Salat. We do not collect account-level personal data such as names, email addresses, or advertising identifiers. Some location-based features may send your GPS coordinates to third-party services to calculate prayer times or resolve city names.

Information We Do Not Collect

أذان الصلاة: Athan al Salat does not collect account-level personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any data for advertising purposes

Local Data Storage

أذان الصلاة: Athan al Salat stores the following data locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. These settings are saved on-device, although location-based features may also send your GPS coordinates to third-party services when needed:

  • Location: GPS coordinates and city name, cached for prayer time calculation and display
  • Prayer Settings: Calculation method, athan sounds, notification and iqama preferences
  • Prayer Cache: 30-day offline prayer time cache
  • Quran: Bundled Arabic text, translation preferences, cached translations, bookmarks, and font size
  • App Settings: Language, dark mode, and onboarding status
  • Purchase Status: Record of in-app purchases

Network Requests

The following is the complete list of network requests the app may make. No direct identifiers such as your name, email address, or device ID are attached to these requests:

  • Aladhan API (aladhan.com) — Used as a fallback only if offline prayer calculation fails. Sends your GPS coordinates and calculation method to calculate prayer times.
  • AlQuran Cloud API (alquran.cloud) — Fetches Quran translations on demand when they are not already cached locally. Sends only the chapter number and translation code.
  • Photon / Nominatim / OpenStreetMap — Used for city search and to resolve your GPS coordinates into a city name for display. No persistent identifier is sent.
  • DNS check (dns.google) — A standard DNS lookup used in the background to check internet availability before refreshing the prayer cache. No application data is sent.

In-App Purchases

أذان الصلاة: Athan al Salat offers optional in-app purchases processed through Samsung Galaxy Store billing using Samsung In-App Purchase (Samsung IAP). We do not receive or store any payment information. Purchase records are handled by Samsung and stored locally on your device as needed for app functionality.

Third-Party Services

أذان الصلاة: Athan al Salat does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services. There is no analytics or advertising SDK. The only external service used for purchases in this Galaxy Store version is Samsung Galaxy Store billing through Samsung IAP.

Children's Privacy

أذان الصلاة: Athan al Salat does not knowingly collect account-level personal information from children or adults.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • You can reset location and preferences from the app's Settings screen

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
